How to Make a PB&J Sandwich!

    • Step by Step:

    1. Gather your ingredients for the sandwich - Peanut Butter, Jelly, Sandwich Bread, Plates, Butter Knife
    2. Pull out two slices of bread.
    3. Open the peanut butter and jelly.
    4. Spread the peanut butter onto one slice.
    5. Spread the jelly onto the other slice.
    6. Combine the two slices.
    7. Cut off the crusts, if desired.
    8. Cut sandwich in half.
    9. Enjoy your sandwich!

    Types of Peanut Butter and Jelly:

    Peanut Butter Jelly
    Smooth Grape
    Crunchy Strawberry
    Almond Butter Mixed Berry
